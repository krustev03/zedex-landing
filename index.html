How It Works

From blank page to live pipeline in 4 steps

A tight, founder-led process. No kickoff decks, no sprawling strategy docs - just a working wholesale system shipped in two weeks.

01

Discovery call

We get on a call and map your ICP, your outreach channels, and what a win looks like. Who you want in your catalog, which regions, store sizes, categories. What's worked and what hasn't.

Day 1 · 45 minutes
02

System build

We source and verify the buyer list, write your sequences in your voice, set up sending infrastructure, CRM sync, and reply handling. You review, we adjust. Everything pre-tested before it goes live.

Week 1 - 2 · Built around your brand

Launch & iterate

The system goes live with a controlled first batch. We watch reply data, deliverability, and buyer reactions in real time - then refine subject lines, angles, and targeting based on what's actually landing.

Once the system is hitting numbers, you choose: we keep operating it monthly and expanding reach, or we document and hand you the keys. Either way, you own the outputs.
