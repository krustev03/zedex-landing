How It Works

From blank page to live pipeline in 4 steps

We get on a call. I scope what you need. Two weeks later you have a system that runs.

01

Discovery call

We get on a call together. I map who you want stocking your products - which retailers, regions, store types, categories - and what's worked vs. what's been stuck.

Day 1 · 15 minutes
02

System build

I find and verify your buyer list, set up the email setup that gets your messages into inboxes (dedicated domains, properly warmed mailboxes, full authentication), connect it to your CRM, and plug in your outreach copy. Everything pre-tested before it goes live.

Week 1 - 2 · Built around your brand
03

Launch & iterate

The system goes live with a small first batch. I watch how it's performing - what's landing in inboxes, what's bouncing, what's replying - and tune the system so your emails land in primary inboxes (not spam) and your pipeline stays clean.

Week 2 - 3 · Live replies in your inbox
04

Scale or hand off

Once the system is hitting numbers, you choose: I keep operating it monthly and expanding reach, or I document everything and hand you the keys. Either way, you own the outputs.

Week 4+ · Your call
