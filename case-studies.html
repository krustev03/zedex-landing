Edible coffee cups REAL CLIENT

Cupffee

Bulgarian sustainable-packaging brand scaling from DTC and pilot food-service contracts into European coffee chains, hotels, and catering distributors.

25×outreach volume vs manual baseline, in 30 days
1/10cost per account contacted
120+qualified buyer replies in the first cycle

The challenge

Cupffee had strong DTC momentum and a handful of food-service pilots, but wholesale outreach to café chains, hotel F&B directors, and catering distributors was bottlenecked at one person pitching manually from a spreadsheet. The category story was strong; the pipeline motion was not.

What I built

  • Verified buyer list of 2,400+ European coffee chains, specialty café groups, and catering distributors - segmented by region and order volume
  • Dedicated sending infrastructure: 6 warmed domains, full DMARC/DKIM/SPF configuration, automated mailbox rotation
  • CRM sync to HubSpot - every touch, reply, and status logged without manual copy-paste
  • Reply routing so positive-intent responses flowed to the founder's inbox; bounces, auto-responders, and out-of-office handled silently
  • Weekly reporting dashboard on sends, landed rates, replies, and accounts opened

The outcome

Inside the first 30 days the system scaled outreach 25× over the manual baseline while dropping cost per account to roughly a tenth. 120+ qualified buyer replies landed; several translated into ongoing European coffee-chain conversations. The founder's calendar moved from chasing lists to closing introductions.

"In one month we scaled our outreach 25× while cutting cost per account to a tenth. Zedex built the system around our category, our buyers, and how we actually sell - not a generic playbook."

Miroslav Zapryanov, CEO & Founder, Cupffee
