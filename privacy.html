Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

This page explains what information I collect, why I collect it, and what you can do about it. I keep this simple on purpose. If anything here is unclear, email me at petar@zedex.dev and I will explain it in plain words.

Who I am

Zedex is run by Petar Krastev, registered in Bulgaria. I am the person who handles your data. You can reach me at petar@zedex.dev. This site lives at zedex.dev.

What I collect

  • Your email address, when you sign up for a free skill or my updates.
  • Booking details, when you book a call. The call is booked through Calendly, which collects your name, email, and the time you pick.
  • Basic site data, like the pages you visit and rough location, through standard web logs and any analytics I use. This is not tied to your name.

I do not collect anything you do not give me, and I do not ask for sensitive information.

Why I use it

  • To send you the free skill you asked for.
  • To send you new skills and short, useful notes about what is working with AI. You can stop these anytime.
  • To set up and run our call if you book one.
  • To understand which parts of the site are useful, so I can make it better.

Who I share it with

I do not sell your data. Ever. I use a few trusted tools to run the basics, and your data passes through them only so they can do their job:

  • An email service provider, to store your email and send you what you signed up for.
  • Calendly, to book calls.
  • My website host and analytics, to keep the site running and see what works.

Each of these has its own privacy terms. I pick tools that take data seriously.

Where your data lives

Some of these tools are based outside the EU. When your data is handled outside the EU, it is done under terms meant to keep it protected to EU standards.

How long I keep it

I keep your email for as long as you want my updates. If you unsubscribe or ask me to delete it, I remove it. Booking records are kept only as long as I need them for our work together.

Your rights

Under EU data rules (GDPR), you can:

  • Ask what data I hold about you.
  • Ask me to correct it.
  • Ask me to delete it.
  • Unsubscribe from my emails at any time, with one click.
  • Complain to your local data protection authority if you think I got it wrong.

To do any of these, email petar@zedex.dev. I will sort it out quickly.

Cookies

This site uses only the basics needed to run it and to see which pages get used. Nothing creepy. Your browser lets you block cookies if you prefer.

Changes to this policy

If I change how I handle data, I will update this page and change the date at the top.
